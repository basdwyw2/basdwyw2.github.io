About Sex At Oxbridge

The author began anonymously writing about her love life in 2010 when she started the blog, Sex At Oxbridge. The blog gained immediate attention across the United Kingdom and later that year she was named as one of the forty bloggers who really count by The Times. Her blog and writing has been featured in most major UK publications, including The Saturday Times, The Sun, Cosmopolitan, Grazia, Glamour, The Daily Mail and The Evening Standard.
